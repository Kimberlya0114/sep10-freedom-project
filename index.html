Context

  • Medical equipment has been around for hundreds of years, but the MRI machine has only been around for a few years, so it still has some issues that can be resolved, but it has also made many improvements thanks to technology.
  • The first MRI machine was created by Raymond Damadian in 1997
  • MRI stands for Magnetic resonance imaging
  • MRI machines are used in the medical industry to help doctors get precise imaging of the inside of someone's body
  • It is very important for MRI machines to be safe because in the United states alone it gets used over 40 Million times in a year.

Problem

MRI scans can transmits radiation to someone when it is being used.

This is a bad thing because radiation can cause health problems like cancer and cardiovascular diseases
